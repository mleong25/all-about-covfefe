Pour Over

A traditional, manual method that passes hot water over ground beans for a period of time in order to extract a smooth, subtle cup of coffee.

Background

The pour over comes from a manual method of brewing coffee by passing water through beans for a certain amount of time which manipulates the coffee's strength and profile. This form of brewing is extremely simple and captures the essense of brewing coffee.
