French Press

A sophisticated method of brewing that permeates water via plunging [pressing] to produce a purer flavor.

Background

The [French] Press method comes from utilizing a pressing mechanism where water is steeped through beans for about 3 minutes before pressing the coffee through a filter. This method is preferred since due to the preservation of the natural oils in the coffee which makes for a bolder profile. This form of brewing is often viewed as elaborate but requires very little manual work.

How to Make

Estimated Time: 4 minutes

    Tools & Ingredients:
  • French Press or other press-type device
  • hot water
  • freshly ground beans
